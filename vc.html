Voice Chat:

Since May 2024, the ReinServer Network supports voice chat. We highly encourage you to use this new form of communication for a more seamless ReinServer experience.

For SJI members, you will have to install the Fabric version of this mod to get rid of the "Connection reset" error on the server. We don't know why it works, but for the time being, it is the only way to play on the server.
