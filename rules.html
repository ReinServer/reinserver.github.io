Network Rules:

"follow them or you'll be permabannedTM"


As always when running a server, we need to have rules to ensure a smooth and fair experience for all. As such, please follow these rules, or you risk a ban.


Global Rules:

These rules apply to all servers, except the Anarchy Server.


• Do not join any server with a hack client.

• Do not spam chat or voice chat.

• Do not send any unfavourable or inappropiate messages in chat or in voice chat.

• Do not attempt to undermine the server's efforts to provide every player a fair playing experience, by either duping rare items, keeping illegal items or attempting to lag or crash the server.

• Do not attack or harrass any player, be it in-game or external. Racism is not tolerated.

• Do not spoof or hack into other people's accounts.

• Do not create and play on another account to bypass your ban.

• Do not scam people for real-life money.

• Do not be an asshole.

• These rules are not exhaustive. Please have common sense and remember that rule breaks are at the discretion of the admins.

ReinServer Anarchy Rules:

These rules only apply to the Anarchy server. Global rules do not apply unless specified otherwise.


• You are allowed to hack, PvP, dupe and KOS on the server.

• You are not allowed to abuse any glitch or exploit on the server that allows game-breaking exploits, such as backdoors, operator status, and obtaining illegal items such as bedrock and enchantment levels above normal obtainment.

By playing on the ReinServer Network, you agree to follow all the rules posted above. Failure to follow these rules may result in a warn, mute, kick, temporary/permanent ban or IP ban.
