firstly, check on reinserver.com/servers if the server is online.

if it is online: try these steps:

• Ensure that the IP and port is correct. For java, use play.reinserver.com. For bedrock, use play.reinserver.com as the IP and 19132 as the port.

• If you've ensured that the IP and port is correct, make sure that your device is connected to the internet.

• If your device is connected to the internet, try joining other Minecraft servers.

If you can join other servers:

Join our Discord or WhatsApp for extra help

If you cannot join other servers:

• Restart Minecraft

• Restart your computer/device

• Restart your router

• Disable your firewall

• Use a vanilla client (if you're running a modified client)


If the above still didn't work, join our Discord or WhatsApp for more hepl.
